# References

## Primary MTMC architecture papers

1. **Cheng-Che Cheng, Min-Xuan Qiu, Chen-Kuo Chiang, and Shang-Hong Lai.** 2023. *ReST: A Reconfigurable Spatial-Temporal Graph Model for Multi-Camera Multi-Object Tracking.* Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV 2023). Also available as arXiv:2308.13229. Source file: `papers/ReST - A Reconfigurable Spatial-Temporal Graph Model for Multi-Camera Multi-Object Tracking.pdf`.

2. **Yizhou Wang, Tim Meinhardt, Orcun Cetintas, Cheng-Yen Yang, Sameer S. Pusegaonkar, Benjamin Missaoui, Sujit Biswas, Zheng Tang, and Laura Leal-Taixé.** 2025. *MCBLT: Multi-Camera Multi-Object 3D Tracking in Long Videos.* arXiv:2412.00692. Source file: `papers/MCBLT - Multi-Camera Multi-Object 3D Tracking in Long Videos.pdf`.

3. **Martin Engilberge, Ivan Vrkic, Friedrich Wilke Grosche, Julien Pilet, Engin Türetken, and Pascal Fua.** 2025. *One Graph to Track Them All: Dynamic GNNs for Single- and Multi-View Tracking.* arXiv:2507.08494. Source file: `papers/One Graph to Track Them All - Dynamic GNNs for Single and Multi-View Tracking.pdf`.
